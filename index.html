Shigesato Itoi

Summary

Shigesato Itoi is a renowned Japanese copywriter, essayist, and game designer, most famously known for being the creator of the MOTHER series.

Creative Visionary Innovative

Life and Timeline

  • 1948: Itoi was born in Maebashi, Japan.
  • 1980: Itoi began his career as a copywriter.
  • 1989: Itoi worked on the first "MOTHER" game for the the Nintendo Famicom.
  • 1994: "MOTHER 2", also known as "EarthBound" in America, releases on the Super Famicom/SNES.
  • 2003: Itoi launched his first popular lifestyle website, which was called "Hobo Nikkan Itoi Shinbun", or Hobonichi.
  • 2006: Itoi released "MOTHER 3" in Japan for the Game Boy Advance, which never officially came to America.
  • 2013: Itoi published his book of essays "Words and Their Stories".
  • 2015: Itoi's work as a designer for Hobonichi Techno had become globally recognized.
  • 2020: Itoi expaned his website to include more of his personal essays.
  • Present: Itoi continues to work on his writing and managin Hobonichi, as of October 4th, 2024.
